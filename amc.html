Rules, May Slipper Day (19th May 2026)

Stand N°4

Teams:
Make 2 teams of 3 players.

Game Modes:
Choose one mode:
-Monarchy / Democracy
-Replace Countries

You can also:
- Start with placing countries first, then do Monarchies/Democracies;
- or only play one category.

Music Rounds:
Music 1 – Revision
Play the first song from the playlist.
Students study the map and try to remember where the countries are.
Music 2 – Country Placement
Play the second song.
Students must replace the countries correctly on the map.
Music 3 – Countries and/or Governments
Play the third song.
Students continue placing countries and/or classify them as:
Monarchies
Democracies

Notes:
- Multiple rounds can be played.
- Teams can change strategy between rounds.
- The team with the most correct answers wins.
