Rules, May Slipper Day (19th May 2026)

Stand N°4

1 - First, make two teams of students

2 - Then take the first song from the playlist ( QR code below ) and play it during the duration of the first half where students read the map and try to remember where each countries goes ( MAP 1 )

3 - After the end of the song, play the next song, now the students must select a difficulty from the cups (Easy, Hard or Government (Monarchies or Democracies) and try to place back the paper pieces on the map

You don’t have to do everything, and you can play multiple rounds in the same category.
